RULES

Please read the following rules and click on Start A


Part A


  • - When you press start, your screen will display the numbers 1 to 25 randomly scattered on your screen
  • - Touch the numbers in order from 1 to 25
  • - If you make a mistake, do not stop, continue to complete the test
  • - The time (sec) it takes you to touch the numbers in the correct order will be recorded
  • - Once you have completed Part A, you will move on to Part B

Part B


  • - When you press “Continue to Part B”, your screen will display the numbers 1 to 13 AND the letters A to L randomly scattered on your screen
  • - Alternate touching the numbers and letters in order. E.g. 1, A, 2, B, 3, C… 12, L, 13
  • - If you make a mistake, do not stop, continue to complete the test
  • - The time (sec) it takes you to touch the numbers in the correct order will be recorded
  • - By pressing “Continue to Part B” you will start the second part of the test. Start immediately.

When you make a CORRECT connection, the preceding number/letter will turn green Once you press start your test will begin immediately. You will not be able to start again
